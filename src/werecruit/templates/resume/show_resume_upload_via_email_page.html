You can upload resumes to your WeRecruit account by

  • Sending resumes as one or more attachments ( docx,pdf file types) from your registered email account to resumes@werecruit.cloud.

  • WeRecruit detects the candidate name, phone, email from the attached resumes to create a record in the system. You should verify the name, email & phone fields and correct them if required.

  • Resumes uploaded can be browsed similar to resumes uploaded from web app by navigating to 'Resumes->Browser' menu.
