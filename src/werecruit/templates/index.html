WeRecruit is on a mission to
    Provide life time affordable resume bank for every recruiter on this planet ( each person on this planet has a business and a personal email. Similarly every recruiter should have WeRecruiter account to create his personal resume bank ).
    Build a world class resume to job matching engine using AI.
    Making collaboration a breeze for all stakeholders ( recruiter, interview panel, hiring manager, HR & candidate ) involved in recruiting for a job position.
    Provide data driven insights to remove bottlenecks and overall improve time to hire.
    Provide an open source recruitment stack.

If you're a freelance recruiter, a small/medium sized organization hiring for self or your clients, we would like you to be part of this journey.

Features available now
Job Positions
  • Create unlimited number of open positions capturing basic information about a job position like Title, Description, Location, Min/Max compensation range etc.
  • Define a team of interview panels, Hiring manager & HR to collaborate seamlessly for every job position.
  • Large library of out of box job application status codes to track each application status.
  • Take notes as you update the job application status.
    • Resumes
  • Upload resumes using web App or send them as email attachment.
  • Auto detect key fields like candidate's name, phones or email from uploaded email.
  • Auto shortlisting of resumes to open job position based on skills match.
  • Directly shortlist resumes sent via email for a job position.
  • Create your personal searchable resume bank.
  • Standard email templates for notifications and reminders.
    • Dashboard & Reports
  • Real time dashboard showing funnel for every active job position.
  • Client wise summary report.
  • Job wise summary report.
    • Roadmap coming soon
  • Collaboration space for recruitment team.
  • Publish job positions to customer's website, linkedIN, weRecruit job Board & third party job boards.
  • Custom tagging on resumes.
  • Email blast to all candidates in your resume bank.
  • Integrated assessment platform.
  • Schedule meetings.
  • Engagement recipes for candidates who have accepted the offers but are yet to join.
  • Social & legal background verifications.
  • Offer letter generation & eAcceptance.
  • digiLocker integration ( India specific )
