WeRecruit is on a mission to build the world's most sophisticated open source recruitment stack in collaboration with recruiters across the globe.
If you're a freelance recruiter, a small/medium sized organization hiring for self or your clients, we would like you to be part of this journey.
Features
Create unlimited job positions.
Define a team of interview panels, Hiring manager & HR to collaborate seamlessly per job position.
Large library of out of box job application status codes to track each application status.
Take unlimited notes as you update the job application status.
Upload resumes using web App or send them as email attachment.
Auto detect key fields like candidate's name, phones or email from uploaded email.
Directly add resumes sent via email to a Job position pipeline.
Create your personal searchable resume bank.
Standard email templates for notifications and reminders.
Collection of summary and detailed reports to communicate effectively with your clients. Real time dashboards to help you focus on things that matter.
